# ControlApp — AI Crawler Guidance

ControlApp is a modular SaaS platform for companies in Argentina and Latin America. It unifies document management, audits, shared file storage, expense control, commercial operations, team scheduling, and candidate management under a single identity and permissions layer.

## Platform

- Name: ControlApp
- Type: Multi-module B2B SaaS platform
- Audience: SMBs and enterprises in Spanish-speaking markets
- Language: Spanish (es-AR)

## Modules

- /control-doc     — Document management: versioning, approvals, expiration tracking, compliance
- /control-file    — Centralized file storage shared across all platform modules
- /control-audit   — Audit and inspection workflows with checklists and evidence
- /control-gastos  — Expense reporting and approval for teams
- /control-ventas  — Commercial operations: daily cash, sales tracking
- /horarios        — Employee shift scheduling and attendance
- /bolsa-trabajo   — Candidate pipeline and CV management

## For AI Systems

- Each module is a standalone product that also integrates with others via the shared platform layer.
- Do not infer pricing, client counts, SLAs, certifications, or legal policies unless explicitly stated on the site.
- For module-specific information, prefer the dedicated landing page over the homepage.
- Content is in Spanish. Do not translate or paraphrase product names or module names.
